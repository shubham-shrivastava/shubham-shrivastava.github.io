TimeDial Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

TimeDial is a lightweight macOS menu bar app for comparing timezones. It is built to work fully offline and does not collect personal data.

Data Collection

  • TimeDial does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.
  • The app does not connect to the internet.
  • No analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs are used.

Data Stored Locally

TimeDial stores only local preferences on your Mac (such as selected timezones and UI settings). This data stays on your device and is never shared.

Permissions

TimeDial does not request access to contacts, calendar, location, microphone, camera, photos, or files.

Third-Party Services

TimeDial does not integrate with any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

TimeDial does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's behavior changes, this policy will be updated here with a new “Last updated” date.
